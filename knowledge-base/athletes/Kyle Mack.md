# Athlete Profile: Kyle Mack

## Summary
- **Discipline(s):** Big Air Snowboard, Slopestyle Snowboard
- **Hometown:** West Bloomfield, Michigan
- **Home Resort:** Park City Mountain Resort, Utah
- **Born:** April 16, 1998
- **Turned Pro:** 2016
- **Stance:** Goofy

## Career Highlights
Kyle Mack is one of the most accomplished big air snowboarders in US history. He won a silver medal in the inaugural Olympic snowboard big air event at the 2018 Pyeongchang Winter Olympics, becoming one of the first athletes to podium in the discipline on the Olympic stage. Mack is a multiple X Games Aspen big air medalist and a consistent FIS Snowboard World Cup big air and slopestyle podium finisher. His goofy stance and powerful, progressive trick combinations — including one of the earliest competitive executions of a cab triple cork — have made him a recognized technical innovator in the discipline.

## Career Progression
- **2015–2016:** Competed on the FIS Snowboard World Cup big air circuit as an amateur. Won multiple junior-level events and earned his first senior World Cup podium finishes.
- **2016:** Turned professional. Named to the US Olympic long team in big air and slopestyle.
- **2017:** Won FIS Snowboard World Cup big air event. Named to the US Olympic Team for Pyeongchang. Won X Games Aspen big air bronze.
- **2018:** Won Olympic silver in snowboard big air at the Pyeongchang Winter Olympics — the inaugural Olympic big air event. Competed in slopestyle as well. Won X Games Aspen big air silver in the same season.
- **2019–2021:** Won multiple FIS Snowboard World Cup big air podiums. Won X Games Aspen big air silver. Trained extensively at Park City Mountain Resort with the US Ski & Snowboard big air team.
- **2022:** Competed at the Beijing Winter Olympics in big air and slopestyle. Won X Games Aspen big air bronze. Podiumed at Dew Tour big air at Copper Mountain.
- **2023:** Regular World Cup competitor and X Games Aspen finalist. Continued training at Park City Mountain Resort.

## Competition Circuit
Mack competes primarily in big air with slopestyle as a secondary:
- **X Games Aspen** (Buttermilk Mountain / Aspen Snowmass) — multiple big air medals
- **Winter Olympic Games** — silver medal (2018 Pyeongchang, big air)
- **FIS Snowboard World Cup** — big air and slopestyle circuits, multiple podiums
- **US Grand Prix** (Park City Mountain Resort) — regular competitor
- **Dew Tour** (Copper Mountain) — medalist

## Sponsorships
Burton Snowboards (primary), Monster Energy, Oakley, Red Bull

## Performance Notes
Mack's goofy stance gives him a distinct competitive perspective on jump features, and his cab-direction tricks — particularly cab triple corks — are among the most technically advanced in the field. He trains year-round at Park City Mountain Resort, which maintains one of the best big air jump facilities in North America for the US Ski & Snowboard team. Mack is known for his measured competition strategy: he often lands both earlier runs safely before unleashing his maximum-difficulty run on the final attempt.
