# Athlete Profile: Julia Marino

## Summary
- **Discipline(s):** Slopestyle Snowboard, Big Air Snowboard
- **Hometown:** Westport, Connecticut
- **Home Resort:** Mammoth Mountain, California
- **Born:** June 29, 1997
- **Turned Pro:** 2016
- **Stance:** Regular

## Career Highlights
Julia Marino is one of the most technically accomplished women's slopestyle and big air snowboarders in the world. She won an Olympic silver medal in snowboard slopestyle at the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics, one of the most decorated results for a USA Snowboard woman in the discipline's Olympic history. Marino has multiple X Games medals across slopestyle and big air and is a consistent FIS Snowboard World Cup podium finisher. Known for her athletic background — she played golf at the national junior level before committing fully to snowboarding — Marino brings a precision-athlete mindset to trick execution and competition strategy.

## Career Progression
- **2014–2015:** Competed as a junior on the FIS circuit while also maintaining an elite junior golf career. Made the difficult decision to focus on snowboarding full-time.
- **2016:** Turned professional. Competed in first senior FIS Snowboard World Cup events and demonstrated immediate technical progression on rail sections.
- **2017–2018:** Established herself on the World Cup slopestyle circuit with consistent top-10 finishes. Competed at X Games Aspen for the first time.
- **2019:** Won first FIS Snowboard World Cup slopestyle podium. Began developing big air as a primary second discipline, training with the US Ski & Snowboard big air team at Park City Mountain Resort.
- **2020–2021:** Broke through with multiple World Cup podiums in both slopestyle and big air. Named to the US Olympic Team for Beijing. Won X Games Aspen big air silver.
- **2022:** Won Olympic silver medal in snowboard slopestyle at the Beijing Winter Olympics with a score of 87.68, behind only Zoi Sadowski-Synnott of New Zealand. A landmark result for USA Snowboard women. Went on to win Dew Tour slopestyle gold at Copper Mountain.
- **2023:** Won X Games Aspen slopestyle silver. Competed on the full FIS World Cup circuit. Began coaching mentorship roles within the US Ski & Snowboard development program.

## Competition Circuit
Marino competes across both slopestyle and big air disciplines:
- **X Games Aspen** (Buttermilk Mountain / Aspen Snowmass) — multiple slopestyle and big air medals
- **Winter Olympic Games** — silver medal (2022 Beijing, slopestyle)
- **FIS Snowboard World Cup** — slopestyle and big air circuits, multiple podiums
- **Dew Tour** (Copper Mountain) — gold and podium finishes
- **US Grand Prix** (Park City Mountain Resort) — regular competitor and podium finisher
- **Burton US Open Snowboarding Championships** (Stratton Mountain, VT) — top-five finishes

## Sponsorships
Salomon Snowboards (primary), Oakley, Monster Energy, Monster Energy, Zumiez

## Performance Notes
Marino is distinguished by her precise rail technique and creative feature usage, stemming in part from her athletic background in golf. She scores particularly well in the rail sections of slopestyle courses, often earning the highest marks among competitors through technical variety. On jump features, she consistently executes frontside and backside 900s and 1080s with clean grabs. She trains primarily at Mammoth Mountain and Park City Mountain Resort, working with the US Ski & Snowboard slopestyle coaching staff.
