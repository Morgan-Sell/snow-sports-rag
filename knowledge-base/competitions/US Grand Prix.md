# Competition: US Grand Prix

## Overview
- **Sanctioning Body:** US Ski & Snowboard (USSS) / FIS
- **Disciplines:** Snowboard Halfpipe (W/M), Snowboard Slopestyle (W/M), Snowboard Big Air (W/M), Freeski Halfpipe (W/M), Freeski Slopestyle (W/M)
- **Frequency:** Annual (January–February)
- **Venue:** Park City Mountain Resort, Utah (primary); Mammoth Mountain has also hosted
- **Prize Money:** Competitive professional purse
- **FIS Points:** Awarded (FIS World Cup event)

## Format & Judging
The US Grand Prix uses FIS judging criteria across all disciplines. It functions simultaneously as a domestic qualification event for the US Olympic Team selection and an international FIS World Cup competition. Athletes from across the world compete alongside US team members. The Park City Mountain Resort venue — home of the US Ski & Snowboard High Performance Center — gives US athletes home-mountain advantage and familiarity with the superpipe and slopestyle venue infrastructure.

## Significance
The US Grand Prix is the most direct domestic pathway to Olympic team selection. US Ski & Snowboard uses Grand Prix performance as a primary selection criterion for the national Olympic team. Athletes who do not achieve qualifying marks at international FIS World Cup events can demonstrate form through the Grand Prix to support their case for selection. The event at Park City Mountain Resort typically draws the deepest US team field of any domestic competition.

## Historical US Champions (Selected)

### Snowboard Halfpipe — Women
- **2023:** Chloe Kim (USA) — dominant multi-year champion
- **2022:** Chloe Kim (USA)
- **2016:** Chloe Kim (USA) — early landmark win at Mammoth Mountain

### Snowboard Halfpipe — Men
- **Multiple years:** Shaun White (USA) — won US Grand Prix halfpipe multiple times during his career

### Snowboard Slopestyle — Women
- **2022:** Hailey Langland (USA) — silver at Park City
- **2021:** Julia Marino (USA)

### Snowboard Big Air — Men
- **2018:** Chris Corning (USA) — first competition win of his backside triple cork 1440
- **2022:** Kyle Mack (USA)

### Freeski Slopestyle — Men
- **2023:** Alex Hall (USA)
- **2022:** Nick Goepper (USA)

## Olympic / FIS Qualifier Status
The US Grand Prix is a full FIS Snowboard and Freeski World Cup event. Results award FIS World Cup ranking points directly affecting Olympic qualification. US Ski & Snowboard additionally uses Grand Prix results as a domestic qualifier for Olympic team selection, making it doubly significant for US athletes.
