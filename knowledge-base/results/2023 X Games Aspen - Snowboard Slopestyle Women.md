# 2023 X Games Aspen — Snowboard Slopestyle Women Results

## Event Details
- **Date:** January 26, 2023
- **Venue:** Buttermilk Mountain, Aspen Snowmass, Colorado
- **Discipline:** Snowboard Slopestyle — Women
- **Conditions:** Mild, with soft snow on the jump features and slightly variable conditions on rails

## Podium
1. Zoi Sadowski-Synnott (NZL) — 88.00
2. **Julia Marino** (USA) — 85.75
3. Tess Coady (AUS) — 83.50

## US Team Performance
**Julia Marino** (USA) took silver with a technically polished run that earned the highest rail section scores among all competitors. Marino, who trains primarily at Mammoth Mountain and Park City Mountain Resort, executed a demanding rail section including a cab-270-out on the first feature and a backside boardslide-to-270-out on the second rail, then landed frontside 900 and cab 1080 on the jump features. Her silver was consistent with her 2022 Olympic silver in slopestyle and confirmed her status as one of the sport's most reliable podium performers.

**Jamie Anderson** (USA) finished 5th, posting competitive jump scores but leaving points on the table in the rails section. Anderson, whose home resort is Palisades Tahoe, expressed satisfaction with her overall run despite the result outside the podium.

**Hailey Langland** (USA) finished 7th, posting one of her more consistent competition performances of the early season. Langland trains at Mammoth Mountain and has been developing her slopestyle and halfpipe programs simultaneously under US Ski & Snowboard coaching.

## Season Standings Impact
Julia Marino's silver at X Games Aspen, added to FIS World Cup results, kept her in contention for a top-three World Cup slopestyle season ranking. Sadowski-Synnott's gold affirmed her position as the world's top-ranked women's slopestyle rider.
