# 2022 Dew Tour — Freeski Slopestyle Men Results

## Event Details
- **Date:** December 11, 2022
- **Venue:** Copper Mountain Resort, Colorado
- **Discipline:** Freeski Slopestyle — Men
- **Conditions:** Cold and firm; consistent wind on the jump features throughout finals

## Podium
1. **Nick Goepper** (USA) — 88.50
2. **Alex Hall** (USA) — 87.25
3. Birk Ruud (NOR) — 85.75

## US Team Performance
**Nick Goepper** (USA) won the Dew Tour freeski slopestyle gold medal at Copper Mountain Resort in a top-two US finish that signaled an exceptionally strong start to the season for the US freeski slopestyle program. Goepper, whose home resort is Steamboat Springs, delivered a powerful run featuring a switch 1440 and multiple double cork combinations across the three jump features. Goepper's rail technique — always a strong point — earned high marks on both feature elements, and his total score of 88.50 edged out his younger teammate.

**Alex Hall** (USA) earned silver just behind Goepper, posting the highest style score of the event but receiving slightly lower amplitude marks in the jump section. Hall, a Park City Mountain Resort native, demonstrated the relaxed precision that has made him the most aesthetically admired freeskier in the discipline. The silver at Copper Mountain set up what would become a season-long head-to-head between Hall and Goepper — ultimately resolved at X Games Aspen in Hall's favor.

## Season Standings Impact
Goepper's gold and Hall's silver at the season-opening Dew Tour gave the US freeski slopestyle program the early lead in the discipline's World Cup rankings. Both athletes maintained top positions through the remainder of the season.
