# 2023 US Grand Prix — Snowboard Big Air Men Results

## Event Details
- **Date:** February 12, 2023
- **Venue:** Park City Mountain Resort, Utah
- **Discipline:** Snowboard Big Air — Men
- **Conditions:** Clear and cold; firm landing zone in the morning; softening through finals

## Podium
1. **Chris Corning** (USA) — 92.50
2. Marcus Kleveland (NOR) — 90.00
3. **Kyle Mack** (USA) — 88.25

## US Team Performance
**Chris Corning** (USA) won the US Grand Prix big air gold at his primary training venue, Park City Mountain Resort, with his signature backside triple cork 1440 — the trick he first landed in competition at a previous Grand Prix and which remains the highest-difficulty jump in his technical arsenal. Corning, who grew up in Silverthorne, Colorado and trains at Breckenridge alongside Red Gerard, posted the most technically demanding run in the field to take gold. His score of 92.50 was a season best.

**Kyle Mack** (USA) earned bronze with a Cab triple cork attempt that was well received by judges for its execution despite slightly lower amplitude than Corning's run. Mack, who trains at Park City Mountain Resort, was competing on home snow — a factor that coaches noted as visible in his comfort level and approach run confidence.

**Red Gerard** (USA) competed and finished 4th, posting a strong slopestyle-style big air run but finishing just outside the podium. Gerard, training at Breckenridge, expressed motivation from the near-miss result.

## Season Standings Impact
Corning's gold and Mack's bronze at the US Grand Prix confirmed US strength in big air heading into the FIS Snowboard World Cup season final. Both athletes were positioned in the top five of the World Cup big air season standings.
