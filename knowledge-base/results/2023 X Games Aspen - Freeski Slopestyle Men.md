# 2023 X Games Aspen — Freeski Slopestyle Men Results

## Event Details
- **Date:** January 27, 2023
- **Venue:** Buttermilk Mountain, Aspen Snowmass, Colorado
- **Discipline:** Freeski Slopestyle — Men
- **Conditions:** Overcast but dry; stable wind conditions across all jump features

## Podium
1. **Alex Hall** (USA) — 92.00
2. **Nick Goepper** (USA) — 89.75
3. Birk Ruud (NOR) — 88.00

## US Team Performance
**Alex Hall** (USA) claimed his second consecutive X Games Aspen slopestyle gold medal with a run that judges and commentators immediately labeled the best freeski slopestyle run in X Games history. Hall, a Park City Mountain Resort native who trains at the US Ski & Snowboard High Performance Center at Park City, combined switch double cork 1260 and a switch misty 1620 across his three jump features, performing each with the characteristic effortless style that has made him the defining aesthetic figure in modern freeski slopestyle. His score of 92.00 was the highest in the event's history.

**Nick Goepper** (USA) earned silver with a technically demanding run featuring a switch 1440 and double cork combinations. Goepper, whose home resort is Steamboat Springs, executed his run with the powerful, confident style that has defined his career since his 2014 Olympic debut. The silver was his best X Games result in several years and confirmed his continued relevance at the top of the discipline approaching the 2026 Olympic cycle.

## Season Standings Impact
Hall's gold and Goepper's silver made X Games Aspen 2023 a clean American sweep of the top two spots in men's freeski slopestyle. Both athletes entered the FIS Freeski World Cup Finals as the top two-ranked US men in the discipline.
