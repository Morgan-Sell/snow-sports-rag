# 2022 Dew Tour — Snowboard Slopestyle Women Results

## Event Details
- **Date:** December 10, 2022
- **Venue:** Copper Mountain Resort, Colorado
- **Discipline:** Snowboard Slopestyle — Women
- **Conditions:** Early season; firm packed snow; course in good shape for December competition

## Podium
1. **Julia Marino** (USA) — 84.75
2. Zoi Sadowski-Synnott (NZL) — 83.50
3. **Hailey Langland** (USA) — 80.25

## US Team Performance
**Julia Marino** (USA) opened her 2022–2023 season with a Dew Tour gold medal at Copper Mountain Resort, the event's permanent home venue. Marino's winning run showcased her signature rail precision across the course's two rail features, then clean frontside 900 and backside 1080 trick executions on the three jump features. As an FIS World Cup event, the gold awarded crucial World Cup ranking points that would factor into the end-of-season standings.

**Hailey Langland** (USA) earned bronze in her best result of the early season. Langland, who trains at Mammoth Mountain, was praised by coaches for the consistency she showed across multiple runs in the qualifying and finals format. The bronze confirmed her position as a regular US team slopestyle podium contributor.

**Jamie Anderson** (USA) finished 5th, posting competitive jump section scores but losing ground to Marino and Sadowski-Synnott in the rail sections. Anderson, based at Palisades Tahoe, remained a top-five competitor heading into the main competition season.

## Season Standings Impact
The Dew Tour at Copper Mountain is the first significant FIS World Cup points opportunity of each season. Julia Marino's gold placed her at the top of the early-season slopestyle rankings, establishing the tone for her title defense campaign.
