# 2023 X Games Aspen — Snowboard Big Air Men Results

## Event Details
- **Date:** January 26, 2023
- **Venue:** Buttermilk Mountain, Aspen Snowmass, Colorado
- **Discipline:** Snowboard Big Air — Men
- **Conditions:** Cold and clear; ideal takeoff conditions throughout

## Podium
1. **Red Gerard** (USA) — 91.50
2. Marcus Kleveland (NOR) — 89.75
3. **Chris Corning** (USA) — 87.00

## US Team Performance
**Red Gerard** (USA) won his first X Games Aspen big air gold medal with a run featuring a cab double cork 1080, demonstrating his expanding technical repertoire beyond slopestyle into big air. Gerard, who grew up in Silverthorne, Colorado and trains primarily at Breckenridge Ski Resort, delivered a run with signature casual style that earned not only the highest score but the loudest crowd reaction of the event. His gold in big air, combined with medals in slopestyle earlier in the week, made him the standout US snowboarder of X Games 2023.

**Chris Corning** (USA) claimed bronze with his signature backside triple cork 1440 — one of the highest-difficulty tricks in competitive snowboarding. Corning, who also trains at Breckenridge Ski Resort and grew up alongside Gerard in Silverthorne, posted the highest difficulty score of any athlete in the event but received slightly lower execution marks, resulting in the bronze. The result was his third X Games Aspen big air medal.

**Kyle Mack** (USA) finished 4th — just off the podium — with a technically demanding run. Mack, who trains at Park City Mountain Resort, posted a cab triple cork in his final attempt but could not surpass Corning's score.

## Season Standings Impact
Red Gerard's gold and Chris Corning's bronze confirmed US dominance in men's snowboard big air. Both athletes entered the FIS World Cup big air season final as leading contenders for the discipline's overall globe.
