# 2022 Burton US Open — Snowboard Slopestyle Women Results

## Event Details
- **Date:** March 5, 2022
- **Venue:** Stratton Mountain Resort, Vermont
- **Discipline:** Snowboard Slopestyle — Women
- **Conditions:** Variable spring conditions; rail features in excellent shape; jump takeoffs slightly wet

## Podium
1. **Julia Marino** (USA) — 86.50
2. Zoi Sadowski-Synnott (NZL) — 85.00
3. Tess Coady (AUS) — 81.75

## US Team Performance
**Julia Marino** (USA) won the Burton US Open slopestyle title in her best result of the 2021–2022 season. The victory was especially meaningful given the venue — Stratton Mountain Resort in Vermont, the home of the US Open — and the timing, coming just weeks after her Olympic silver medal in Beijing. Marino's winning run featured her characteristically precise rail technique with a cab-270-out and complex backside combinations, followed by solid frontside 900 and cab 1080 jump tricks. Judges gave her the highest rail section score of any athlete in the event, rewarding the technical difficulty and creative execution that has become her trademark.

Marino, who trains primarily at Mammoth Mountain, expressed that the US Open gold represented a personal highlight alongside her Olympic silver — the two achievements together confirming her status as the top women's slopestyle snowboarder in the world at the time of competition.

**Jamie Anderson** (USA) competed but finished outside the top three, dealing with a fall on her stronger run. Anderson, whose home resort is Palisades Tahoe, acknowledged it was not her best event performance of the season.

## Season Standings Impact
Julia Marino's US Open gold, as an FIS World Cup event, boosted her final FIS Snowboard World Cup slopestyle season ranking to a top-five result — among the best of her career.
