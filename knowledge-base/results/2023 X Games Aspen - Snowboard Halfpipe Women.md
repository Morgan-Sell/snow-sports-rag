# 2023 X Games Aspen — Snowboard Halfpipe Women Results

## Event Details
- **Date:** January 27, 2023
- **Venue:** Buttermilk Mountain, Aspen Snowmass, Colorado
- **Discipline:** Snowboard Halfpipe — Women
- **Conditions:** Sunny, cold; firm pipe surface in morning, softening in finals

## Podium
1. **Chloe Kim** (USA) — 90.00
2. Kokomo Murase (JPN) — 86.00
3. Yuka Fujimori (JPN) — 82.50

## US Team Performance
**Chloe Kim** delivered a dominant performance to claim her fourth consecutive X Games Aspen halfpipe gold medal, extending her record as the most decorated women's halfpipe athlete in X Games history. Kim's winning run featured back-to-back Cab 1080s followed by a frontside 1080, executed with the signature amplitude that places her consistently 20+ feet above the pipe lip. Her score of 90.00 was her highest of the season to that point. Kim, who trains primarily at Mammoth Mountain and serves as the anchor of the US Ski & Snowboard halfpipe team, dedicated the victory to her coaching team.

**Hailey Langland** (USA) competed in her secondary halfpipe discipline, finishing 5th. Her improving halfpipe performance across the 2022–2023 season reflects the dual slopestyle/halfpipe development program she has been running at Mammoth Mountain.

## Season Standings Impact
Chloe Kim's X Games Aspen victory confirmed her position as the dominant force in women's halfpipe entering the second half of the FIS World Cup season. Though X Games does not award FIS points, the result signaled to rivals that Kim's form was peak heading into the final World Cup events and the season's championship events.
