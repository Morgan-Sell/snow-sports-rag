# 2022 Burton US Open — Snowboard Halfpipe Women Results

## Event Details
- **Date:** March 4, 2022
- **Venue:** Stratton Mountain Resort, Vermont
- **Discipline:** Snowboard Halfpipe — Women
- **Conditions:** Spring conditions; soft snow; the pipe maintained well given warm temperatures

## Podium
1. **Chloe Kim** (USA) — 91.00
2. Queralt Castellet (ESP) — 86.25
3. Maddie Mastro (USA) — 83.00

## US Team Performance
**Chloe Kim** (USA) won the Burton US Open halfpipe title just weeks after her historic back-to-back Olympic gold at the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics. Kim's performance at Stratton Mountain Resort demonstrated that her Olympic run had not depleted her competitive motivation — she arrived and dominated with authority. Her winning run featured the signature trick combination that has defined her career: Cab 1080 to frontside 1080 with massive amplitude. The crowd at Stratton's halfpipe reacted with a standing ovation to what was arguably the most celebrated post-Olympic result in recent women's halfpipe history.

Kim credited the Burton US Open with special emotional significance — as a longtime Burton athlete and someone who considers the US Open part of snowboard culture's foundation, the Stratton win was particularly meaningful to her.

**Maddie Mastro** (USA) again earned bronze, consistent with her X Games Aspen result earlier in the season. The result confirmed her position as a reliable US team contributor in halfpipe.

## Season Standings Impact
Chloe Kim won the 2021–2022 FIS Snowboard World Cup halfpipe season globe by a wide margin. The Burton US Open gold, as an FIS World Cup event, added to that margin and concluded her dominant Olympic season on the highest possible note.
