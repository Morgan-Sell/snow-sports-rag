# 2022 X Games Aspen — Snowboard Halfpipe Women Results

## Event Details
- **Date:** January 21, 2022
- **Venue:** Buttermilk Mountain, Aspen Snowmass, Colorado
- **Discipline:** Snowboard Halfpipe — Women
- **Conditions:** Cold and clear; the 2022 X Games Aspen was held without fans due to COVID protocols

## Podium
1. **Chloe Kim** (USA) — 93.25
2. Queralt Castellet (ESP) — 87.75
3. Maddie Mastro (USA) — 82.50

## US Team Performance
**Chloe Kim** (USA) won her third consecutive X Games Aspen halfpipe gold medal in dominant fashion, posting the highest halfpipe score by any woman in X Games history at the time. Kim's winning run included back-to-back Cab 1080s and a massive frontside 1080, performed with amplitude and consistency that drew admiration from the entire field. The 93.25 was a personal-best score and confirmed her form entering the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics — where she would go on to win gold weeks later.

**Maddie Mastro** (USA) earned bronze — her first X Games halfpipe medal — with a progressive run featuring a frontside 900 and backside 720 combination.

**Hailey Langland** (USA) competed in the halfpipe as a secondary discipline, reaching the final but finishing outside the podium. Her continued development in the halfpipe reflects the US Ski & Snowboard program's investment in multi-discipline athlete profiles.

## Season Standings Impact
Kim's X Games gold was the final tune-up before the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics, where she won gold just weeks later. The performance affirmed that she was at peak form for the Olympic season.
