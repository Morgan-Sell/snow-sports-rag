# 2022 US Grand Prix — Snowboard Halfpipe Women Results

## Event Details
- **Date:** February 5, 2022
- **Venue:** Park City Mountain Resort, Utah
- **Discipline:** Snowboard Halfpipe — Women
- **Conditions:** Cold and clear; high-altitude conditions at Park City's 9,000+ ft superpipe venue

## Podium
1. **Chloe Kim** (USA) — 94.00
2. Queralt Castellet (ESP) — 87.50
3. **Hailey Langland** (USA) — 80.75

## US Team Performance
**Chloe Kim** (USA) set the tone for her 2022 Olympic season with a dominant US Grand Prix gold at her primary training venue, Park City Mountain Resort. Kim's score of 94.00 was the highest of her pre-Olympic competition period and featured the back-to-back 1080 combination she would carry to Olympic gold in Beijing weeks later. The win at the US Grand Prix was especially meaningful given that Park City is where much of her Olympic preparation had taken place under US Ski & Snowboard coaching.

**Hailey Langland** (USA) claimed bronze in the halfpipe — her best US Grand Prix halfpipe result. Langland, who trains at Mammoth Mountain, has been developing her halfpipe program as a complement to her primary slopestyle discipline, and the Park City podium showed the development was gaining traction.

## Olympic Selection Impact
The US Grand Prix at Park City Mountain Resort served as the final domestic Olympic qualifier before the 2022 Beijing Games. Results here were critical for athletes seeking to confirm Olympic team spots. Kim's dominant performance left no doubt about her Olympic selection. Langland's bronze placed her among the top US halfpipe competitors and informed US Ski & Snowboard's team selection decisions.
